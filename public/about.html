About BitOverFlow



Bit Overflow is a month long Open source program conducted by ISTE AEC Student's Chapter under event Techxom in collaboration with AEC Coding Club. It is a program aimed at helping beginners to gain exposure, knowledge and experience of the world of Open Source Development. This will also act as the perfect opportunity to get acquainted with the world of GIT and GITHUB. Participants get a chance to work on various kinds of projects under the guidance of experienced mentors.
